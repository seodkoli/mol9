Frequently Asked Questions

How can I identify a gas leak in my home or business?

Signs of a gas leak include a distinctive odor, hissing sounds near gas lines or appliances, dead vegetation near gas lines, and physical symptoms such as dizziness or nausea. If you suspect a gas leak, evacuate the area immediately and call a Gas Fitter for assistance.

How often should I have my gas appliances serviced by a Gas Fitter in Hobart?

It's recommended to have gas appliances serviced annually by a qualified Gas Fitter to ensure they are operating safely and efficiently, reducing the risk of breakdowns or hazards.

What should I do if I smell gas in my home or business in Hobart?

If you smell gas, leave the area immediately, avoid using any electrical appliances or switches, and call your gas supplier's emergency line and a licensed Gas Fitter for assistance.
